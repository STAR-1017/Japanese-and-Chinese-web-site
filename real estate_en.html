Egypt's Real Estate Attractiveness and Economic Development

One of the attractions of investing real estate in Egypt is its low prices. In addition, stable demand for rentals and rising rental income are expected, supported by large domestic demand due to population growth.
In anticipation of population explosion in Cairo and future economic growth, the government of Egypt has announced plans to relocate to the new capital.Currently, construction is underway on an area larger than the 23 wards of Tokyo, and real estate under development there is being put up for sale simultaneously.
Especially "R7" area is an upscale residential area in the heart of the new capital, which will open in phases beginning in 2023. This is an area that investors should not miss, as it is expected to increase property values in the future.
